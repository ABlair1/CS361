How Are The Food Footprints Calculated?

The total emissions produced by each item account for land use changes, feed (for animals), farming operations, processing, transportation, packaging, and retail operations (Nemecek & Poore, 2018). Follow the link in the citation below to read the peer reviewed research that supports the Food Footprint Calculator.

Nemecek, T., & Poore, J. (2018). Reducing food's environmental impacts through producers and consumers. Science, 360(6392), 987-992. https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.aaq0216
